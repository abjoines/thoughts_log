thought.log()

is an experimental microsite to assist you in a sort of mindfulness practice. One mindfulness concept which I’ve come across, put plainly, is to acknowledge and release your thoughts - especially negative ones. This is a place where you can write down and let go of your thoughts. It’s completely impermanent and more convenient than a physical piece of paper.


I am currently ideating on some potential new features - such as a storing your thoughts so they can be filtered through and downloaded.
